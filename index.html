FAQ

Common questions before booking.

Short answers reduce hesitation without overwhelming visitors.

What types of pests do you handle?

We handle common insects such as ants, cockroaches, flies, spiders and bed bugs, plus rodents such as rats and mice. Commercial and food-site pest support is also available.

Are your pest control methods safe for pets and children?

The service is safety-conscious and takes children, pets, pregnant women, plants and sensitive spaces into account. Any site-specific precautions will be explained before or after treatment.

How often should I schedule pest control treatments?

Many homes and businesses use quarterly maintenance. Food-handling or high-risk premises may require more frequent monitoring.

Do you offer services for commercial establishments?

Yes. Organic Pestar supports restaurants, shops, offices, warehouses, food-handling establishments and industrial or commercial properties.

Do you provide pest control certificates?

Yes. Certificates can be provided for council or municipal health inspectors and pest control audit requirements for food industries.
