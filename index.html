Home / Residential Pest Control

Residential pest control is built around protecting the home, family members, pets, plants and food areas while treating visible pest activity and the hidden entry points that allow pests to return.

What this service covers

  • Interior and exterior inspection to identify pest type, nesting points, entry gaps and high-pressure areas.
  • Treatment for cockroaches, rodents, ants, spiders, bedbugs, fleas, ticks, flies, wasps and seasonal pests.
  • Exterior-focused prevention around doors, garages, storerooms, refuse areas, garden edges and possible access routes.
  • Practical advice to reduce repeat activity after the first treatment.

Why it matters

Rain and cold weather can push pests indoors. Once inside, pests can contaminate food areas, leave droppings, damage property and create repeat infestations if entry routes are not controlled.
