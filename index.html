Our Story

About Bloomhaus

Welcome to Bloomhaus Sewing Academy — a creative space where beginners and aspiring makers come to discover the art of sewing. We believe that sewing is a skill anyone can learn, and we're here to make that journey enjoyable, structured, and empowering.

Our 6-week programme is designed to take you from complete beginner to confident maker. Whether you want to create your own clothes, upcycle, or simply discover a new hobby, Bloomhaus is the place to bloom.

Focus

Getting comfortable with the machine · Understanding materials · Control and stitch types

  • Parts of a sewing machine & threading
  • Bobbin winding & insertion
  • Machine safety & basic maintenance
  • Straight, zigzag & backstitching
  • Stitch length & tension
  • Types of fabric & grain line
  • Measuring, marking & cutting
Practical: Practice stitching on paper and fabric, mastering lines, curves, and corners. Sew a Scrunchie or Tote Bag.
Focus

Creating simple patterns · Putting it all together

  • Taking body measurements
  • Drafting a basic pattern & layout
  • Seam allowance & finishing
  • Joining fabric pieces
  • Elastic insertion & hems
Practical: Draft a simple skirt, cut fabric pieces, join, and sew a complete garment.
Focus

Top construction · Flare skirt sewing

  • Drafting a basic top
  • Flare skirt pattern
  • Assembly techniques
Practical: Complete a top and flare skirt from draft to finish.
Focus

Dress pattern drafting · Garment construction · Fitting basics

  • Dress pattern drafting
  • Garment construction
  • Fitting basics
Practical: Sew a complete dress from your own draft.
Focus

Pyjama set drafting · Sewing

  • Top & shorts/pants drafting
  • Elastic waistband
  • Finishing techniques
Practical: Complete a full sleep set — top and bottoms.
Focus

Course review · Final garment assessment

  • Final garment assessment
  • Finishing & presentation
  • Course review & showcase
Outcome: Assessment + Graduation ceremony for qualifying students.
